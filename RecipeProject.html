Menemen

Menemen is a Turkish breakfast recipe that is frequently cooked in many Turkish houses. It's both yummy and high in nutrients -its main ingredients are peppers, tomatoes, and eggs! It tastes much better on summer, when the veggies are on their season, but you may also have it on winter if you wish.
Note: There is great controversy on whether menemen should contain onions or not, but I think it's delicious both ways. So I encourage you to try both and decide which one you like better. :)
How to eat it: The appropriate way of eating menemen is demonstrated in this video.
Enjoy!

Ingredients
green peppers 3
tomatoes 5
eggs 2
olive oil 5 tbsp
salt 1 tsp
onion (optional) half
mozarella (optional) as you wish

Instructions:

  • Chop the peppers (and onions if you prefer) in small pieces.
  • Peel the tomatoes and slice them in small cubes.
  • Put some oil in a pan and pop the peppers (and onions) in it.
  • Fry the peppers a little bit and add tomatoes.
  • Add salt.
  • While the tomatoes are boiling, break the eggs in a bowl and stir until uniform. (If you wish, you can directly break the eggs into the pan later.)
  • When the tomatoes get soft enough, slowly add the eggs and mix well (or break them into the pan).
  • (optional) If you'd like to give it a cheesy touch, you may add some mozarella as well.
  • Just a few moments till the eggs get completely cooked (and the mozarella gets melted) and you're done!
